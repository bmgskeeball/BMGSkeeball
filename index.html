Glossary

W:
Wins
L:
Losses
PF
Points Scored
PA
Points Allowed
PD
Points Differential
BEST
(Top 4 frames over the entire season.)
300+ GAMES/400+ GAMES
300/400 Point (or more) games during the season
ACTUAL
Total Points divided by # of Frames
CAREER AVG
Player's Season Avgs divided by # of seasons
H/L
Season average with highest & lowest score removed
FRAMES
Number of Frames (9 balls per frame) played
AVG BALL
Average Points Scored Per Ball
HDCT
Handicap (Points added to score in team play for competitive balance. Negative handicaps register as '0')
TICKETS
Prize tickets won. 4 tickets per 100. Bonuses for 300+ and 400+ scores.

Rules

Team Play

Each team member plays 10 frames (90 balls). Most team points wins. Top two teams compete for league title.

Awards

Team points championship (most points in a season) Individual points title (Highest average points per frame) Playoff championship (Top 2 teams at end of season) Individual championship (Bracket of all qualifying players over season) Wooden Spoon (Lowest average scoring player over season)
